Who we are

We care your health

We aim to provide you with a healthcare journey focused on your wellbeing. Let us transform your lives through clinical excellence, compassionate care and health education

Smart is beyond being a group of entities providing the full spectrum of healthcare services; it is a pioneering institution that has been continuously thriving for over a decade, to become the reference of excellence and international standards in India.

Smart Hospital

Smart Hospital founded by Dr. Soloman in 2005 and has progressed to establish itself at the forefront of health care market and medical services within the private sector in India with a high success rates comparable to international standards.

In 2008, PM Dr. Manmohan Singh inaugurated the first expansion of our establishment which resulted in doubling our inpatient capacity and the introduction of new divisions such as the open-heart surgery, and new clinics such as neurosurgery, neurology, nephrology and infertility clinics, which positioned Smart as a pioneer establishment in the private healthcare sector in India.

Smart provides services to the adult and pediatric populations in India and the surrounding countries in the Arabian Gulf. Our services include all types of General Surgeries, Cardio and Thoracic Surgeries, Interventional Cardiology, InternalMedicine Sub-Specialties, Medical Radiation and Surgical Oncology, PalliativeCare, Dermatology, Family Medicine, Obstetrics / Gynecology, Pediatric, Dental,Chiropractic, Emergency Services, Ophthalmology, Operating Theatres, Anesthesia, Adult and Pediatric Intensive Care Units, Allergy, Immunology,Respiratory and Sleep Disorders and Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy. It also has the largest neonatal care unit in the Western Province of the Kingdom.

Memorable Time Lines

2006: The first privatehospital in India to be accredited by the Joint Commission International(JCI);

2008: The first privatehospital in India to be accredited by the Australian Council on Healthcare Standards International (ACHSI);

2022: Smart Hospital awarded CBAHI Accreditation for the third time. The certification willbe valid until 31st of December, 2025.
