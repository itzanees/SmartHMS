HEALTH CHECKUP

Health Checkup - Bronze

The Health Checkup Bronze Package is a comprehensive health assessment that includes a range of essential tests. It covers a complete blood count (CBC) to assess overall health, creatinine and uric acid tests to evaluate kidney function, fasting blood sugar (FBS) or random blood sugar (RBS) to check blood glucose levels, blood pressure measurement, electrocardiogram (ECG) to assess heart function and a cholesterol test to evaluate lipid levels.
